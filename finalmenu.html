Fuchka

Fuchka, also known as pani puri or golgappa, is a popular Indian street food consisting of crispy, hollow puris filled with spiced mashed potatoes, chickpeas, and a tangy, spiced water.

Biriyani

Biryani is a classic South Asian mixed rice dish, known for its aromatic spices, tender meat (or vegetables), and fluffy basmati rice.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a classic curried dish with soft tender chunks of char-grilled chicken, simmered in a delicious & super aromatic gravy.

Momos

Momos are steamed dumplings, a popular dish in Tibetan, Nepali, and North Indian cuisine. They typically feature a dough filled with minced meat, vegetables, or a combination of both, seasoned with spices and herbs.

Masala Dosa

A Masala Dosa is a South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with a spiced potato mixture, and served with chutneys and sambar. It's a popular, crispy, and flavorful dish, enjoyed for breakfast or lunch.

Dessert

Dessert is a course that concludes a meal; the course consists of sweet foods, such as cake, biscuit, ice cream, and possibly a beverage, such as dessert wine or liqueur. Some cultures sweeten foods that are more commonly savory to create desserts.

Pizza

Pizza is an Italian, specifically Neapolitan, dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Sandwich

A sandwich is a dish typically consisting variously of meat, cheese, sauces, and vegetables used as a filling between slices of bread, or placed atop a slice of bread.

Rolls

Rolls are a popular grab-and-go snack known for their delicious fillings wrapped in soft bread, paratha, tortilla, or thin pastry sheets. They are flavorful, portable, and often customized with a variety of ingredients.

Burger

A burger is a sandwich made with a ground meat patty, or a meat substitute, served in a sliced bun with various toppings like vegetables and sauces.

Chowmein

Chowmin is a Chinese stir-fried noodle dish made with fried noodles, vegetables, and often meat or tofu, seasoned with a savory sauce.

Rice

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice stir-fried with other ingredients like vegetables, eggs, and meat in a wok or frying pan.
